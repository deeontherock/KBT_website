Kentuckiana Behavior Therapy

Kentuckiana Behavior Therapy provides behavioral therapy services for the Greater Louisville area.

Michael O'Brien is a licensed behavior analyst who enjoys helping families and individuals improve their quality of life by molding problem behaviors into healthy habits.

    KBT specializes in helping families with problem behaviors concerning:
  • Toilet Training
  • Picky Eating
  • Social Skills
  • Aggression
  • Self-Harm
  • Screen-Overuse
